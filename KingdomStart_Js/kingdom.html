Kingdom Start

You have decided to start a new kingdom since the rulers of your kingdom are terrible.

To do this, you need to gain villagers for your kingdom.

Answer the questions below to gain villagers and helpers to start make your kingdom.

You have made it to a village of 30 farmers.
They ask you "If we make 120 bundles of wheat and you want to take a fourth of it, how many would you take?".
Answer their question correctly to gain their trust.

You have made it to a village of 15 merchants.
They ask you "What is the cost of 4 apples, 6 pears, and 7 bannans if apples cost 75 cents, pears 50 cents, and bannans 1 dollar?".
Answer their question correctly to gain their trust.

You have made it to a village of 50 builders.
They ask you "If we have 36 people in a village and each building can hold 4 people, how many houses do we need to build to house everyone?".
Answer their question correctly to gain their trust.

You have made it to a village of 20 knights.
They ask you "We are 20 knights and only have 13 swords. Each sword costs 2 rubies. How many rubies do we need to make sure each knight has one sword?".
Answer their question correctly to gain their trust.

You have made it to a village of 35 clothes makers.
They ask you "To make an outfit for an adult, we need 10 yards of cloth and an outfit for a child is 5 yards. We need to make clothes of 10 children and 7 adults but we only have 80 yards of cloth.
How much more cloth do we need to make all of the outfits?".
Answer their question correctly to gain their trust.
