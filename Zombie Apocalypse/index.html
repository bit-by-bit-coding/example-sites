• The goal of this game is to survive and live happily ever after!
  • If you encounter a zombie, you can fight it off with a weapon.
  • If the Plague gets to you, a potion can heal you and bring you back to life!
  • At the market in the village, you can stock up your inventory.
  • Don't go too long without food or water or else you just might perish!
  • If you want to survive, don't let your hunger level reach 40 and don't let your thirst level reach 30!
